Software Freedom Day

20th September 2014, SMVDU

Software Freedom Day is dedicated to the world of Open Source. It is a worldwide celebration of Open Source and Free Software. Being an alma mater of numerous Open Source Contributers and Supporters, SMVDU is going to continue to be a part of SFD 2014, which falls on 20th September this year. In the spirit of Open Source Revolution we will be supporting and spreading the word about Open Source World by conducting a few workshops, competitions and talks during this Software Freedom Day. It's going to be a day full of knowledge, contribution and fun. We are eagerly waiting for you to be a part of Software Freedom Day at SMVDU.
